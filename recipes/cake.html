Cake Recipe

Tasty Cake

This splendid chocolate cake will make your mouth water. The creamy chocolate flavor will make you feel like you're in heaven. The taste of this cake has a nice and rich flavor that will captivate you. This is a must try recipe for any baker.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate Cake Mix
  • Chocolate Pieces
  • Frosting
  • Syrup

Steps

  1. First use the cake mix and put it into oven and bake
  2. When the cake is done baking add the frosting
  3. Next add the syrup on top of the cake
  4. Lastly add the chocolate pieces
