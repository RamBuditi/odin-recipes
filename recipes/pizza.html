Pizza Recipe

Delicous Pizza Pie

This beautiful pizza has nice selection of toppings such as olives and greenpeppers. The pizza has a superb golden crust with an amazing taste that will blow you away. You have to try this recipe to experience one of the best dishes ever made at your home. If you follow the directions correctly you are sure to get an amazing treat.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Green Peppers
  • Olives
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. First prepare the white flour dough
  2. Next add the tomato sauce on top of the dough
  3. After that add the cheese
  4. Lastly add the topics and bake the pizza in the oven and wait half an hour
