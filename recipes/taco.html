Taco Recipe

Amazing Taco

This taco is one of a kind with its great use of using multiple types of vegtables. The vegetables match well with the sauce used in this recipe. Also the cheese adds another layer of flavor that makes this dish even better. Overall this taco is a must try food item and you won't regret eating it.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Tomato
  • Sauce
  • lettuce
  • Tortilla

Steps

  1. First prepare the vegetables by cutting them
  2. Next add the vegetables into the tortilla
  3. Fold the tortilla
  4. Lastly add sauce to the taco
